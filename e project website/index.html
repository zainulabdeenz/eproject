CAKE

Whether enjoyed warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or savored slowly with a cup of coffee, every bite is a blissful escape into a world of sweet, chocolatey perfection. It's a timeless classic that brings joy to both young and old, making any occasion extra special with its irresistible charm.

COOKIES

Cookies are tiny bundles of delight, each bite brimming with nostalgia and comfort. Whether they're warm and gooey right out of the oven or crisp and dunked into a glass of milk, cookies have a universal appeal making them the perfect treat for every mood andthat transcends generations.

>

PASTRIES

Pastries are delightful, bite-sized treasures of the culinary world. With their flaky, buttery layers and a range of sweet or savory fillings, they offer a burst of flavor and texture in every bite. Whether it's a warm, golden croissant for breakfast or an elegant fruit danish for dessert, pastries have an innate ability.

CAKES

Ingredients:



For the Cake :


  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk

COOKIES

Ingredients:



For the Cookies :


  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

PASTRIES

Ingredients:



For the Pastries :


  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (for sweet pastries, optional)
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or white vinegar (for flakiness)
